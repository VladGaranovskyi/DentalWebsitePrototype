The Dentist

Born and raised in Ukraine, Dr. Kopyoff started her career in the Pre-Dentistry program in Odessa State Medical University. She continued to go on and earn a Diploma in the Qualification of Physician-Stomatologist that is the equivalent of DDS in her home country. Dr. Kopyoff interned at a postgraduate program of general dentistry where she earned the Certificate of Doctor Specialist in Therapeutic Dentistry. After moving to the United States, Dr. Kopyoff was accepted to NYU College of Dentistry. During her studies she had earned the Dr. Bernard E. Rudner Memorial award for demonstration superior performance in providing oral comprehensive care and administrating a dental practice.

Our Services

Routine Exams

Routine exams, also known as regular check-ups or periodic health assessments, refer to a series of medical examinations or assessments that individuals undergo at regular intervals to monitor their overall health and detect potential health issues early.

Dental Hygiene

Dental hygiene refers to the practice of maintaining oral health and cleanliness to prevent dental problems and promote overall well-being. It encompasses a range of habits, routines, and practices that individuals follow to keep their teeth, gums, and mouth healthy.

Veneers

Veneers are thin, custom-made shells typically made of porcelain or composite resin that are designed to cover the front surface of a tooth.

Dental Bonding

Dental bonding is a cosmetic dental procedure used to repair or improve the appearance of a tooth.

Dental fillings

Dental fillings are restorative dental materials used by dentists to repair and restore teeth that have been damaged by decay (cavities) or minor fractures.

Emergency dentistry

Emergency dentistry refers to dental care and treatment provided on an urgent basis to address sudden and severe dental problems, injuries, or conditions that require immediate attention to alleviate pain, prevent further damage, or save a tooth.

Dental Bridges

Dental bridges are a type of dental restoration used to replace one or more missing teeth.

Dental Crowns

Dental crowns, also known as dental caps, are custom-made prosthetic devices that cover the entire visible portion of a tooth above the gumline.

Dentures

Dentures is common dental prosthetic option used to address different dental issues, such as missing teeth or cosmetic imperfections.

Teeth whitening

Teeth whitening, also known as dental bleaching, is a cosmetic dental procedure designed to lighten the color of the teeth and remove stains or discoloration.

Implants

Dental implants are artificial tooth roots made of biocompatible materials such as titanium or titanium alloys that are surgically placed into the jawbone beneath the gums.

Root Canals

A root canal, also known as endodontic therapy, is a dental procedure used to treat and save a severely damaged or infected tooth.
